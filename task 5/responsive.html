Ikoma and his comrades fight to reclaim Unato from zombie-like Kabane,

Tomodachi Game

Yūichi and his childhood friends get caught in the
Tomodachi Game after their school trip funds are stolen
due to someone's debt.The game tests their friendship
as they confront the lure of wealth, posing the ultimate challenge to their bonds.

hajimeno ippo

"Hajime no Ippo" depicts Ippo Makunouchi's journey from a
bullied high schooler to a determined boxer, emphasizing themes
of hard work, friendship, and personal growth in his
quest to become the Japanese featherweight champion.

hunter x hunter

"Hunter x Hunter" follows Gon Freecss, a young boy
aspiring to become a Hunter like his absent father,
as he embarks on a perilous journey, forming alliances
and uncovering dark secrets in the pursuit of his goals.

skeleton knight in another world

"Skeleton Knight in Another World" follows Arc, a gamer
transported into a fantasy world as a skeletal knight,
as he navigates challenges, forms alliances, and seeks redemption
for his cursed existence while wielding unique abilities.
